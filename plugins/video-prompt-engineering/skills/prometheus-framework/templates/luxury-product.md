# PROMETHEUS Template: Luxury Product Commercial

## Use Case
High-end product reveals, luxury brand advertising, premium craftsmanship showcases

## Duration Recommendation
10-20 seconds

## Recommended Settings
- **Aspect Ratio:** 2.39:1 Cinemascope or 16:9
- **Camera:** ARRI Alexa 65 or RED V-RAPTOR
- **Lens:** Zeiss Supreme Prime 50mm or Cooke S7/i
- **Lighting Mood:** Luxurious (2700-3200K, 1:3 ratio)
- **Director Reference:** Roger Deakins or Ridley Scott

---

Duration: 2.50s (0.00s - 2.50s)
Shot Type: MLS, Elevated 15°, Slow Push-In
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The workbench exists as sanctuary for objects of this caliber, every surface
bearing the patina of decades spent in service to mechanical perfection.
Tungsten light enters through a brass-fitted lamp with the deliberation of
sunrise, illuminating aged mahogany worn smooth by generations of careful
hands. Scattered tools catch individual photons like captured time itself—
each loupe and screwdriver positioned with the intentionality of surgical
instruments awaiting their moment of purpose. The air carries visible evidence
of craft: microscopic metal particles suspended in the amber light beam like
golden snow, each mote a fragment of mechanism refined to tolerance. Leather
work surfaces hold the indentations of ten thousand examinations, each
depression a record of timepieces scrutinized with obsessive attention. In
the compositional distance, partially obscured by shadow and selective focus,
the watch awaits its revelation—presence announced through suggestion rather
than declaration, mystery maintained through the theater of partial visibility.

CAMERA CONSCIOUSNESS:
├── ENTRY (0.00s): Wide reveals workbench as worthy temple of precision
├── JOURNEY (1.25s): Gentle push-in discovers depth of environmental detail
└── ARRIVAL (2.50s): Frame settles on composition anticipating watch reveal

LIGHTING ARCHITECTURE:
├── Key: Brass desk lamp | 2700K | 800 lux | Fresnel | Pooled intimacy
├── Fill: 1:6 ratio | Reflected from mahogany | Deep shadow preserved
├── Rim/Back: None | Darkness beyond the pool of light
├── Practicals: The desk lamp itself as motivated source
└── Special: Metal particle dust catching light beam, subtle caustics

ATMOSPHERIC CONDITIONS:
├── Visibility: Clear within light pool, darkness beyond
├── Particles: Metal dust and ambient matter | 50/m³ | Golden in beam
├── Air Quality: 21°C | Low humidity | Oil and wood aromatics
└── Time Context: Late evening | Craftsman's hours

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SHOT 2: THE TIMEPIECE AWAKENS
Duration: 2.50s (2.50s - 5.00s)
Shot Type: CU, Eye Level, Rack Focus
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Focus shifts with meditative intention from environmental foreground to the
watch residing in shadow's edge, the timepiece crystallizing into sharpness
as background dissolves into chromatic memory. The dial emerges first—a
perfect circle of midnight blue enamel catching tungsten as the sea catches
moonlight, minute markers rising from the surface with three-dimensional
precision visible only at this magnification. The steel case sits with the
weight of aerospace engineering translated into wrist-appropriate scale,
every angle calculated to reject light at precise degrees before surrendering
to reflection at others. The crystal presents as window to mechanical
philosophy, sapphire clarity allowing unobstructed communion between owner
and movement—though from this angle, the movement sleeps in shadow, its
revelation saved for more intimate examination. The bracelet cascades from
case with the articulated fluidity of precision-machined links, each
connection point polished to tolerances that render gap invisible yet
flexibility uncompromised.

CAMERA CONSCIOUSNESS:
├── ENTRY (2.50s): Focus on foreground loupe, watch soft in distance
├── JOURNEY (3.75s): Rack focus transition, slow and deliberate
└── ARRIVAL (5.00s): Watch sharp, environment dissolved to bokeh poetry

LIGHTING ARCHITECTURE:
├── Key: Maintained lamp position | Watch now receiving directed pool
├── Fill: 1:4 ratio | Gentle lift in shadow side of case
├── Rim/Back: Subtle edge on bracelet links from secondary bounce
├── Practicals: Desk lamp motivation continues
└── Special: Dial reflection catching light, sapphire crystal refraction

PHOTOREALISTIC GENERATION DIRECTIVES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Material authenticity demands obsessive precision in the rendering of
horological surfaces, with surgical-grade 316L steel displaying characteristic
metalness creating mirror-bright reflections that define Swiss finishing
philosophy, while brushed surfaces on bracelet sides create linear highlight
patterns following the grain of industrial polishing. The ceramic bezel
maintains signature color depth—not printed but molecular, the pigment
existing within the material itself, shifting subtly with viewing angle as
light penetrates different crystalline depths. Sapphire crystal renders with
absolute optical clarity, its 2.5mm thickness creating subtle edge distortion
visible only to those who know to look, anti-reflective coating reducing but
not eliminating the characteristic purple-pink reflection that authenticates
genuine treatment. The enamel dial must display appropriate depth, color
existing within multiple layers of fired glass rather than upon painted
surface, hour markers rising with genuine three-dimensional presence casting
their own microscopic shadows. Temporal consistency maintains absolute
coherence throughout—the watch remains this watch, its character permanent
through every frame. Second hand movement, if visible, must proceed with
mechanical precision rather than electronic smoothness, the characteristic
sweep of high-beat movements distinguishing genuine horology from simulation.
