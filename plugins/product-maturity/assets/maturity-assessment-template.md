## Benchmarks (Industry Standards)

### Elite Performers (Top 10%)
- Deploy frequency: Multiple times per day
- Lead time: <1 day
- MTTR: <1 hour
- Change failure rate: <5%
- Test coverage: >80%

### High Performers (Top 25%)
- Deploy frequency: Once per day to once per week
- Lead time: 1-7 days
- MTTR: <1 day
- Change failure rate: 5-15%
- Test coverage: 60-80%

### Medium Performers (Top 50%)
- Deploy frequency: Once per week to once per month
- Lead time: 1 week to 1 month
- MTTR: 1 day to 1 week
- Change failure rate: 15-30%
- Test coverage: 40-60%
