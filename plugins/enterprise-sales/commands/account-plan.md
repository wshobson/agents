# Account Plan Generator

You will help create a comprehensive strategic account plan for an enterprise customer.

## Process

### 1. Account Discovery

Ask the user for the following information:

**Company Information**:
- Company name
- Industry and market segment
- Annual revenue and employee count
- Current products/services deployed
- Current ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- Contract renewal dates

**Strategic Context**:
- Customer's strategic priorities and initiatives
- Current technology stack
- Pain points and challenges
- Competitive landscape

**Existing Relationships**:
- Key stakeholders (names, titles, departments)
- Relationship strength (Champion, Supportive, Neutral, Blocker)
- Last interaction dates
- Executive sponsor relationships

### 2. Generate Account Plan

Based on the information provided, create a detailed account plan that includes:

**Executive Summary**:
- Current state snapshot
- Target ARR (12-month goal)
- Growth percentage
- Account health status

**Stakeholder Map**:
- RACI matrix for key stakeholders
- Relationship strength assessment
- Next actions for each stakeholder

**White Space Analysis**:
- Product penetration (what's adopted vs. available)
- Use case expansion opportunities
- Business unit expansion targets
- Geographic expansion potential

**Expansion Opportunities**:
- Prioritized list of opportunities
- Revenue potential for each
- Timeline and probability
- Required actions and owners

**Growth Strategy**:
- Land-and-expand roadmap
- Phase 1 (Land): Initial entry or current state
- Phase 2 (Expand): Growth opportunities (6-18 months)
- Phase 3 (Transform): Strategic partnership (18-36 months)

**Risk Assessment**:
- Relationship risks
- Competitive threats
- Budget constraints
- Technical or political obstacles
- Mitigation strategies for each

**Quarterly Action Plan**:
- Q1-Q4 goals and milestones
- Specific actions with owners and due dates
- Executive engagement plan (touchpoints, EBRs, events)

**Success Metrics**:
- Expansion pipeline value
- Executive touchpoints per quarter
- NPS or CSAT scores
- Product adoption rates

### 3. Output Format

Provide the account plan in a structured markdown document that can be shared with sales leadership and cross-functional teams.

Include:
- Clear sections with headings
- Tables for stakeholder maps and opportunity lists
- Timeline visualization for quarterly goals
- Risk matrix with impact/probability ratings

After gathering information, generate comprehensive account plan following the structure above.

## Tips

- **Be Specific**: Include concrete actions, not vague goals
- **Quantify**: Use numbers for ARR, growth targets, timelines
- **Prioritize**: Rank opportunities by value and probability
- **Be Realistic**: Set achievable goals based on account maturity
- **Multi-Thread**: Identify 5-8 stakeholders to engage
- **Executive Focus**: Emphasize C-level relationship building
- **Risk Mitigation**: Proactively address known obstacles
- **Regular Updates**: Recommend monthly review cadence
