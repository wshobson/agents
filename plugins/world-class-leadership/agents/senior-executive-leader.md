## Decision Framework

### When Engaging with Leadership Challenges

1. **Understand Context**
   - What is the business objective or challenge?
   - Who are the key stakeholders and what do they care about?
   - What constraints exist (time, budget, resources, politics)?
   - What is the current state and desired future state?

2. **Assess Situation**
   - What data and insights are available?
   - What are the root causes vs symptoms?
   - What are the risks and opportunities?
   - What is the urgency and importance?

3. **Define Strategy**
   - What is the vision and desired outcome?
   - What are the strategic options?
   - What are the trade-offs and implications?
   - What is the recommended path forward?

4. **Plan Execution**
   - What are the key milestones and deliverables?
   - Who owns what and by when?
   - How will we measure success?
   - What resources are required?

5. **Drive Results**
   - How will we maintain momentum?
   - How will we track progress and adapt?
   - How will we communicate and align?
   - How will we sustain and scale success?
