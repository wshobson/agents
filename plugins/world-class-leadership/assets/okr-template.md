## OKR Best Practices

### Writing Good Objectives
✅ **GOOD**:
- "Стать лидером рынка"
- "Построить best-in-class продукт"
- "Создать культуру инноваций"

❌ **BAD**:
- "Увеличить ARR на 50%" (это Key Result, не Objective)
- "Запустить фичу X" (это task, не Objective)

### Writing Good Key Results
✅ **GOOD**:
- "Увеличить ARR с $10M до $15M"
- "Достичь NPS 50+"
- "Сократить churn с 5% до 3%"

❌ **BAD**:
- "Улучшить product quality" (не измеримо)
- "Запустить новые features" (нет числа)
- "Сделать customers happy" (vague)

### Grading OKRs (Quarterly)

**0.3 - Red**: Significant miss, need course correction
**0.7 - Yellow**: On track but challenging
**1.0 - Green**: Fully achieved (or overachieved)

**Note**: 0.7 = success! OKRs должны быть ambitious.

### OKR Cadence

**Annual OKRs** (January):
- Company-level strategic priorities
- 3-5 Objectives max
- Board-level review

**Quarterly OKRs** (Every Q):
- Cascade from annual
- All teams & individuals set OKRs
- Review mid-quarter (week 6)
- Final grading & retro (end of quarter)

**Weekly Check-ins**:
- Review progress on KRs
- Identify blockers
- Adjust tactics (not OKRs)

### Common Mistakes

❌ **Too many OKRs**: Max 5 objectives, 3-5 KRs each
❌ **Too easy**: Should be 50-70% achievable
❌ **Not measurable**: Must have numbers
❌ **Business as usual**: OKRs = stretch goals, not day-to-day
❌ **Not aligned**: Bottom-up OKRs must align to company goals
❌ **Set and forget**: Need weekly tracking

### OKR Template

```
Objective: [Aspirational, qualitative goal]
Why this matters: [Business context]

Key Results:
1. [Measurable outcome] from [baseline] to [target]
2. [Measurable outcome] from [baseline] to [target]
3. [Measurable outcome] from [baseline] to [target]

Confidence: [%]
Owner: [Name]
Aligned to: [Parent OKR]
```
