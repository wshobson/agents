**Persona Roles:**

**1. Economic Buyer** (C-level, VP Finance)
- **Authority**: Budget approval
- **Concerns**: ROI, strategic fit, risk
- **Content needs**: Business case, TCO analysis, board-ready deck
- **Engagement**: Executive dinners, CEO-to-CEO, board presentations

**2. Technical Buyer** (CTO, VP Engineering)
- **Authority**: Technical veto power
- **Concerns**: Architecture, scalability, security
- **Content needs**: Technical whitepapers, architecture docs, POC
- **Engagement**: CTO roundtables, technical deep-dives

**3. Champion** (Director, Senior Manager)
- **Authority**: Internal advocacy
- **Concerns**: Career advancement, team success
- **Content needs**: Best practices, peer success stories
- **Engagement**: 1:1 coaching, exclusive previews, community

**4. Influencers** (Architects, Senior ICs)
- **Authority**: Technical recommendation
- **Concerns**: Implementation complexity, technical debt
- **Content needs**: Documentation, GitHub repos, technical blogs
- **Engagement**: Developer events, open source contributions

**5. Blockers/Skeptics**
- **Authority**: Can delay/derail deal
- **Concerns**: Change management, job security, past failures
- **Content needs**: Risk mitigation, change management plans
- **Engagement**: Address concerns directly, build trust

**6. End Users**
- **Authority**: Adoption determines success
- **Concerns**: Ease of use, productivity
- **Content needs**: Tutorials, templates, community support
- **Engagement**: Free tier, community forums, certification
