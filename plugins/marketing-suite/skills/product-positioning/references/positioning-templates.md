# Positioning Templates от Top Companies

## AWS-Style Positioning Template

```markdown
# [Product Name] Positioning

## One-Liner
[The world's most X and Y solution for Z]

Example: "The world's most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud platform"

## Customer-Obsessed Framing
**For** [specific customer segment]
**Who** [face this critical business challenge]
**[Product]** [delivers this transformational outcome]

## Value Pillars (3-5)
1. **[Pillar Name]**: [One sentence] → Proof: [Metric/customer]
2. **[Pillar Name]**: [One sentence] → Proof: [Metric/customer]
3. **[Pillar Name]**: [One sentence] → Proof: [Metric/customer]

## Competitive Differentiation
Unlike [alternative approach], [Product] [unique capability]

## Trust Indicators
- Customers: [Fortune 500 count, specific logos]
- Scale: [Global presence, regional coverage]
- Innovation: [R&D investment, patents, releases per year]
- Security: [Certifications, compliance]
```

- [Y] frameworks with SDKs
- [Z] platform integrations
```

## Usage Notes

**When adapting templates:**
1. Start with company culture fit (technical vs business, bold vs conservative)
2. Adjust for audience maturity (early adopters vs mainstream)
3. Match competitive intensity (differentiate hard or collaborative)
4. Align with GTM motion (PLG vs sales-led vs partner-led)

**Red flags to avoid:**
- ❌ "Best", "leading", "innovative" without proof
- ❌ Feature lists without benefit translation
- ❌ Ignoring competitive alternatives
- ❌ Same message for all audiences
- ❌ No quantifiable proof points
