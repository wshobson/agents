# Business Model Canvas Generator

Create a comprehensive Business Model Canvas for your startup with strategic guidance on each component.

## Usage

```bash
/business-model-canvas
```

This command creates an interactive Business Model Canvas that helps you visualize and validate your entire business model on one page.

## Output

Generates a complete BMC with strategic recommendations for each of the 9 building blocks.
