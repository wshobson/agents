# Шаблон Pitch Deck (Sequoia Format)

## Slide 1: Company Purpose
**One-liner что вы делаете**

```
[Company Name]
[Tag line в 1 предложение]

Example: "Stripe - Payments infrastructure for the internet"
```

## Slide 2: Problem
**Какую проблему решаете**

- Pain point #1 (с количественной оценкой)
- Pain point #2 (с конкретным примером)
- Pain point #3 (с emotional impact)

**Ключевой вопрос**: Насколько это "hair on fire" problem?

## Slide 3: Solution
**Ваше решение**

- Product screenshot или demo
- Ключевые возможности (3-5)
- Почему это 10x better?

**Ключевой вопрос**: Насколько это лучше альтернатив?

## Slide 4: Why Now
**Почему это сейчас, а не 5 лет назад?**

- Market shift (technology, regulation, behavior)
- Timing factors
- Why are we well-positioned?

**Примеры**:
- Uber: Smartphones + GPS + payments
- Zoom: Remote work trend + cloud infrastructure
- OpenAI: AI breakthrough + compute cost decline

## Slide 5: Market Size
**TAM / SAM / SOM**

- **TAM** (Total Addressable Market): Весь рынок
- **SAM** (Serviceable Available Market): Ваш segment
- **SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market): Realistic capture

**Example**:
- TAM: $50B (global cloud infrastructure)
- SAM: $5B (Russia + CIS)
- SOM: $500M (target segments in 5 years)

**Обязательно**: Bottom-up validation (not just analyst reports)

## Slide 6: Product
**Product demo / screenshots**

- User journey
- Key workflows
- Differentiated features
- "Magic moment"

**Tip**: Live demo > screenshots > mockups

## Slide 7: Traction
**Рост метрик**

- Revenue / ARR (если есть)
- User growth (MoM или YoY)
- Retention / engagement
- Key milestones hit

**Chart format**:
- Hockey stick growth
- Cohort retention curves
- Month-over-month growth rate

**Ключевой вопрос**: Убедительный product-market fit?

## Slide 8: Business Model
**Как зарабатываете деньги**

- Revenue model (subscription, usage-based, transaction fee)
- Pricing tiers
- Unit economics:
  - CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
  - LTV (Lifetime Value)
  - LTV:CAC ratio (target: 3:1)
  - Payback period (target: <12 months)
- Path to profitability

## Slide 9: Competition
**Competitive landscape**

- 2x2 matrix positioning
- Direct competitors
- Indirect alternatives
- Your unfair advantage

**Positioning**:
- Don't say "we have no competitors"
- Show you understand market
- Highlight differentiation
- Explain moat / defensibility

## Slide 10: Team
**Почему именно вы победите**

- Founders background
- Relevant experience
- Domain expertise
- Key hires
- Advisors / investors

**Highlight**:
- Previous exits
- Deep domain knowledge
- Technical capabilities
- Complementary skills

## Slide 11: Financials
**3-year projections**

| Metric | 2025 | 2026 | 2027 |
|--------|------|------|------|
| ARR    | $2M  | $10M | $40M |
| Customers | 50 | 200 | 800 |
| Team size | 20 | 60  | 150 |

**Include**:
- Revenue forecast
- Key assumptions
- Burn rate
- Path to profitability (or next milestone)

## Slide 12: The Ask
**Сколько привлекаете и зачем**

- Round size: $[Amount] Series A
- Use of funds breakdown:
  - 50% - Engineering (product dev)
  - 30% - Sales & Marketing (GTM)
  - 20% - Operations (hiring, infra)
- Milestones to achieve in 18-24 months
- Next round expectations

**Example**:
"Raising $15M Series A to:
- Double engineering team (10 → 20)
- Scale sales from $2M → $15M ARR
- Expand to 3 new markets
- Target: $15M ARR, 150+ customers by Series B"

## Appendix Slides
- Detailed product roadmap
- Go-to-market strategy
- Customer testimonials / case studies
- Detailed competitive analysis
- Team bios
- Financial model details

## Tips для успешного pitch

1. **Storytelling**: Начните с compelling story
2. **Data-driven**: Подкрепляйте каждое утверждение данными
3. **Focus on problem**: Проблема важнее solution
4. **Show traction**: Growth > everything else
5. **Know your numbers**: Знайте каждую метрику наизусть
6. **Be authentic**: Будьте честны о challenges
7. **Create FOMO**: Показывайте momentum
8. **Rehearse**: Отрепетируйте 10+ раз

## Red Flags (чего избегать)

- ❌ "We have no competitors"
- ❌ "Conservative projections" с 2x ростом
- ❌ Top-down TAM без bottom-up validation
- ❌ Слишком много slides (max 15-20)
- ❌ Мелкий шрифт / захламленные слайды
- ❌ Технический jargon для бизнес-презентации
- ❌ Отсутствие traction / product-market fit proof
